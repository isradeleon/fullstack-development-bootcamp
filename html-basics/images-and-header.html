Most widely used formats

JPEG/JPG: The most widely used in WEB. It doesn't support transparency but provides a good balance between quality and file size.


PNG: Generaly larger than the JPG format due to lossless compression, but still widely used. Supports transparency (ideal for logos & icons).


SVG: Vectorized images are infinitely scalable. They support transparency & are light weight. Not as widely supported as JPGs & PNGs.


WEBP: Launched by Google in 2010. Generally lighter & faster to load than PNG/JPG, but not as widely supported.
